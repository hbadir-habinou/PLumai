PlumAI

1. Nom de la solution

Nous proposons le nom PlumAI.

Justification :

  • "Plum" refers to African plums, the core of our project.
  • "AI" highlights the use of artificial intelligence for classifying plums into six categories: unaffected, bruised, cracked, rotten, spotted, unripe.
  • The name is concise, memorable, and fitting for the JCIA Hackathon 2025.

3. Définition de la solution

PlumAI leverages a convolutional neural network (CNN) to automatically classify African plums into six quality and defect categories: unaffected, bruised, cracked, rotten, spotted, and unripe. Trained on the Kaggle african-plums-quality-and-defect-assessment-data dataset, it predicts plum quality from images with high accuracy.

4. Objectifs du projet

Objectif principal : Create an automated tool to assist farmers and industries in sorting African plums by quality, reducing manual effort and boosting efficiency.

Objectifs spécifiques :

  • Develop a precise CNN model for six-category plum classification.
  • Achieve at least 80% accuracy on validation data.
  • Deliver a simple interface for image-based plum classification.
  • Meet JCIA Hackathon 2025 requirements (GitHub submission, video, form).
